Week 4: Clustering

Here are some thoughts on the exercises from Week 4:
This week's exercises are all about categorizing atomic clusters based on some descriptors. This is a very important task in Computational Physics, as it reduces highly complex systems (in many dimensions) to a few choice dimensions, which can be visualised and understood.
